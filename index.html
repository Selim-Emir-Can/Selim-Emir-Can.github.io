About Me

Hi, my name is Selim Emir Can! I am a rising Junior studying electrical engineering at UCLA. I am passionate about mechatronics and am currently conducting research in the Robotics and Mechanisms Laboratory (RoMeLa). Outside of school, I like making personal projects, tutoring, playing basketball and table tennis.

Robotics Research

Gas Sensor Shell

I designed a gas sensor shell to 1) store an array of sensors oriented correctly relative to the gas flow 2) have enough space to accomadate wiring, a PCB and a headless Raspberry Pi 3) have a tower-like structure to add/remove sensors if needed.

2 in 1 Spatula



I designed a spatula to 1) hold and/or flip food by clamping it between two surfaces 2) act as a scraper spatula or a flipper spatula depending on use 3) control it using servos and an Arduino for any cooking process.
