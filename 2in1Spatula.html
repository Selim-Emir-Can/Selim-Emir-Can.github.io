2 in 1 Spatula

I designed a spatula to 1) hold and/or flip food by clamping it between two surfaces 2) act as a scraper spatula or a flipper spatula depending on use 3) control it using servos and an Arduino for any cooking process.

Design Process

Design Specifications

The specifications given to me for this project were the following:

  • The product must be able to flip food such as burgers, pizza slices, sandwiches, etc...
  • The product must make it easy and reliable for the cooking robot to lift up and scrape food from the pan.
  • The product must be lightweight and be able to lift 1kg of food.

Initial Design Considerations

Initially, two dual axle motors for the flipper spatula and the clamp was going to be used. In this design, the motors for the flipper spatula and the clamp would be stacked, making the design more compact compared to the current design. However, after some research I came to the conclusion that the dual axle motors were too heavy, not strong enough, or too expensive. Furthermore, the current design uses less material because the clamp is placed in between the flipper spatula.
